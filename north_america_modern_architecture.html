Comcast Center {Philadelphia, USA}{North America}

  • Height: 297 meters
  • Date construction started: 2005
  • Date finished: 2008
  • Cost: US$ 540 million
  • Owners: Liberty/Commerz 1701 JFK Boulevard L.P.
  • Engineers and architects: Thornton Tomasetti and Robert A. M. Stern Architects
  • The skyscraper has 1,238,000 square feet (115,000 m2) of rentable space.

Torre Mayor {Mexico City} {North America}

  • Height: 225 meters
  • Date construction started: 1999
  • Date finished: 2003
  • Cost: US$ 230 million
  • Owners: George Sorosa
  • Engineers and architects: WSP Group and Adamson Associates Architects
  • Built with 96 dampers, which work like car shock absorbers to block the resonating effect of the lakebed and its own height.
