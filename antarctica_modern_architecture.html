Halley VI {Antarctica}

  • Date construction started: 2005
  • Date finished: 2012
  • Cost:£26 million
  • Owners: British Antarctic Survey (BAS)
  • Engineers and architects: AECOM and Hugh Broughton Architects
  • Provides fully serviced accommodation for 32 people.

SANAE IV {Antarctica}

  • Date construction started: 1991
  • Date finished: 1997
  • Cost: USD $ 36 million
  • Owners: South African National Antarctic Program
  • Engineers and architects: SANAP engineers and architects
