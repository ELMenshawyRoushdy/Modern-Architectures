Burj Khalifa {United Arab Emirates, Dubai} {Middle East}

  • Height: 829.8 meters
  • Date construction started: 2004
  • Date finished: 2009
  • Cost: USD $ 1.5 billion
  • Owners: Dubai city
  • Engineers and architects: Bill Baker and Adrian Smith
  • The design of Burj Khalifa is derived from patterning systems embodied in Islamic architecture

Royal Clock Tower {Makkah, Saudi Arabia}{Middle East}

  • Height: 581.1 meters
  • Date construction started: 2004
  • Date finished: 2011
  • Cost: $ US 15 billion
  • Owners: Government of Saudi Arabia
  • Engineers and architects: Dar Al-Handasah Architects and Mahmoud Bodo Rasch
  • The building is topped by a four-faced clock, visible from 25 kilometres (16 miles) away. The clock is the highest in the world at over 400 m (1,300 ft) above the ground. The clock faces are the largest in the world, surpassing the Cevahir Mall clock in Istanbul.
